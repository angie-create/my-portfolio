A mobile app that helps online shoppers visualize how outfits look on them using AI.

Objective

To create a seamless and innovative online shopping experience by enabling users to visualize how outfits will look and fit on their bodies through AI technology.

The Challenge

Clara AI addresses the uncertainty and frustration of online shopping, where users struggle to ensure outfits fit well and suit their style. Comparing options is challenging, and frequent returns waste time, making it hard to shop with confidence.

"How might we help users quickly find outfits they like that match their personal style and body type?

The Solution

Clara AI is a mobile app that helps users visualize how outfits look on them using AI. Designed for busy online shoppers, it simplifies finding, trying on, and buying clothes with confidence.
